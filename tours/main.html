Explore Tours

If you haven't figured it out already, we're a space tourism company. So we do tours.

Currently, we offer the following three tours:

The WorldWide Tour™

Involves three hours in space at a height of 90km above the earth's surface. During this time, you will revolve around the Earth one time.

The Zenith Tour™

Involves a day in space at the height of 300km above the earth's surface. During this time, you will orbit around the Earth, living like an astronaut.

The ISS Tour™

The most elevated experience we offer. Involves three days in space, two of which will be spent abroad the International Space Station.

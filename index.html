The Experience

Put simply, you can now tour the expanses of space.

We at Odyssey offer you a chance to escape your comfort zone and explore something about which many of us have wondered: space. Through our three tours, we seek to give everyone a chance to finally travel to space in a safe, exhilarating, and relatively inexpensive manner.

The View

There's nothing like space, and its endless mysteries and questions.

A beautiful blue and green sphere, with swirling clouds and shimmering land. The sight of city lights at night, the majesty of mountains and deserts, and the vastness of the oceans. That's Earth. Space offers a unique perspective on our planet, highlighting both its beauty and fragility. And now you can experience this yourself.
